  **The Dragon Suite Vision**

*What is Dragon Suite and why does it exist?*

Dragon Suite is an agentic AI-powered platform that bridges the gap between talent, learning, and opportunity in India and globally. The core idea is simple: stop asking people to write resumes and start letting them prove their skills through verified certifications, real assessments, and actual project work.

The platform serves three audiences simultaneously — aspiring candidates who want to upskill and get hired, companies and recruiters who want to find verified talent fast, and educational institutions that want to track and improve placement outcomes.

Dragon Suite is built around a self-reinforcing flywheel:

* Candidates learn and practice on Dragon Prep

* Top performers qualify for Dragon Elite

* Dragon Agent autonomously finds and applies to matching jobs

* Companies discover verified talent through Dragon Hire

* Assessment data feeds back into Dragon GPS to sharpen career navigation

* Dragon Bridge connects every part of the cycle for recruiters and placement officers

**🧭  Dragon GPS**

*AI Career Navigation Engine — Your live, adaptive career map*

Dragon GPS is the career intelligence engine at the heart of Dragon Suite. It does not give generic advice — it builds a live, personalized roadmap from where a candidate is today to the exact role and salary they want to reach, then adapts that map in real time as the candidate progresses and as market demand shifts.

The analogy is a GPS for your career: just like a driving navigator recalculates the route when you miss a turn or when traffic changes, Dragon GPS reroutes your learning path when you progress faster than expected, when market demand spikes in a new area, or when a better opportunity appears.

**Three Route Options**

| Route | What It Means |
| :---- | :---- |
| Fastest Path — Intensive Sprint | Aggressive, full-time commitment. Daily 4-hour blocks. Reaches the goal in 3 months. Best for those who want results fast and can dedicate full time. |
| Balanced Path — Steady Progress | Work-life balanced approach. 2-hour daily commitment with weekend deep dives. Reaches the goal in 6 months. Best for working professionals. |
| Deep Mastery — Thorough Scholar | Comprehensive understanding with portfolio projects and mentorship. 1.5 hours daily over 12 months. Best for those who want to deeply understand every concept. |

**The Journey Map**

Every candidate has a personalized Journey Map that shows:

* Completed milestones (e.g., Python foundations, Deep Learning fundamentals)

* The current active module with week-by-week progress

* Upcoming modules with estimated start dates

* Certification targets with ETAs

* The final goal — a specific job role — with a countdown

**Daily Micro-Actions**

Each day, Dragon GPS generates a specific set of micro-actions — short, focused tasks that keep the candidate on track. These are not generic suggestions; they are precisely calibrated to the candidate's current weak spots, upcoming certification requirements, and available time. Examples include watching a specific tutorial, solving two practice problems, or building a mini-project.

**Real-Time Market Signals**

Dragon GPS constantly monitors the job market and surfaces signals like which skills are surging in demand, which are declining, and what the market is currently paying. If a skill the candidate is learning is suddenly surging in demand, the GPS may recommend accelerating that module. If a related skill would add significant hiring value for minimal extra effort, it proactively suggests a reroute.

**The Rerouting Intelligence**

If a candidate is progressing faster than expected, Dragon GPS may recommend adding additional skills to their path — for example, suggesting Agentic AI patterns if the candidate is ahead on RAG topics — showing exactly how many extra weeks that adds and how much it increases the match score for top-tier roles. The candidate can accept or decline the reroute.

**7-Layer Proctoring System**

Dragon Arena's proctoring is its most technically ambitious feature. Every assessment generates a live Trust Score that combines 7 independent signals:

* Face Detection and Recognition — verifies identity is maintained throughout the test

* Eye-Gaze Tracking — detects sustained patterns of looking off-screen

* Lip Movement Detection — identifies if the candidate is speaking to someone

* Multi-Person Detection — flags if additional people enter the camera frame

* Browser Tab and Window Monitoring — logs all tab switches or window changes

* Audio Anomaly Detection — analyzes background voices and suspicious sounds

* Phone and Secondary Device Detection — identifies if the candidate is using another device

Additional anti-cheating measures include AST-based code plagiarism detection for coding tests, IP and device fingerprinting, AI-generated unique question variants so no two assessments are identical, and time anomaly detection.

**🎯  Dragon Hire**

*B2B Assessment Platform — For Enterprises*

Dragon Hire is the enterprise-facing version of Dragon Arena. While Dragon Arena is the candidate experience, Dragon Hire is the platform that companies use to create, deploy, and analyze assessments. The key promise to enterprises is: zero leaked questions, AI-calibrated assessments that are unique every time, and a 7-layer trust score on every candidate.

**The Core Problem Dragon Hire Solves**

Traditional hiring assessments fail in two ways. First, questions get leaked and shared online, making the test meaningless after a few months. Second, assessments are not calibrated to the actual company's context — a generic Python test does not measure whether a candidate can solve the specific problems that company faces. Dragon Hire solves both: AI generates unique calibrated assessments every time, and the generation is guided by the company's own context.

**4-Dimensional Assessment Calibration**

Every assessment Dragon Hire generates is calibrated across four dimensions:

* Company Context — questions are tailored to the industry, scale, and tech stack of the specific company

* Role Specificity — the difficulty and focus areas match what the role actually requires day-to-day

* Skill Calibration — questions are benchmarked against real-world performance data from thousands of previous assessments

* Level Precision — the seniority level of the role is reflected in question complexity and expected depth of answers

**⚒️  Dragon Forge**

*Content Engine — The Engine Room*

Dragon Forge is the backend content engine that powers everything else in Dragon Suite. It is an admin-only platform that harvests content from 127 external sources, processes and de-duplicates it, and uses AI to generate original, unique content — the questions, problems, case studies, and learning modules that every other module depends on.

**The Core Idea**

The insight behind Dragon Forge is that the most valuable asset in an assessment and learning platform is not the technology — it is the content. And the only way to stay ahead of question leakage and to cover the breadth of 43 professional domains across 10 exam categories is to generate content at machine speed. Dragon Forge uses a seed-and-generate approach: it harvests patterns and metadata from existing sources, then uses AI to create entirely original content calibrated to those patterns.

**The Ingestion Pipeline**

Content flows through a six-stage pipeline:

* Crawl / API — 127 sources are continuously scraped or queried

* Clean and Parse — raw content is structured and normalized

* Deduplicate and Validate — near-duplicates are removed (34% deduplication rate)

* Tag and Classify — every piece of content is tagged by domain, difficulty, exam type, and topic

* Quality Gate — automated quality checks filter out low-quality content

* Vector Store — approved content is indexed as vector embeddings for AI retrieval

**AI Generation Pipelines**
